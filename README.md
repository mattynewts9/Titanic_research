By Gender:

Women had a much higher survival rate than men

Around 70% of women survived compared to only 20% of men

By Passenger Class:

Survival was closely linked to social class

Over 60% of first-class passengers survived

In contrast, only about 20% of third-class passengers survived

Being in first class dramatically increased your chances of survival

By Age:

Passengers, especially people aged 10-40 years old survived.

Older passengers had the lowest survival rates

Key Takeaways
Being female significantly improved your chance of survival

Being in first class gave you a major advantage

Being younger, meant you were more likely to survive
